The Supra MK4(1993-2002)

Introduction: The A70 Supra took a giant leap forward in terms of performance, design, and technology, becoming one of the most loved and recognized sports cars of its time.

Engine: It offered the famous 3.0L 2JZ-GTE inline-six engine in the turbocharged version. The 2JZ-GTE engine was praised for its tuning potential and strength,
making the A70 Supra one of the most potent cars on the market.

Key Features: The A70 had a sleek and more refined look compared to previous models. It came with advanced features like digital climate control, multi-link suspension,
and a solid body structure. Its turbocharged variant could hit 0-60 mph in under 5 seconds, which was a performance benchmark at the time.
